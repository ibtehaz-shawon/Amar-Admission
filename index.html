Our Privacy policy explains the following:

  1. What we collect personal information and why we collect it.
  2. How we use those information.
  3. The choices we offer and how you can access and update it.

Information we collect :

  1. Information we collect: We collect your mobile number, name and detailed information of your Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations.
  2. Why we collect it: In order to provide better and correct result for your inquiry, we collect your information.

How we use those information:

  1. We use the information collected to provide you the correct result. We DO NOT save your Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations in our databases.
  2. We may use your device's local storage to save your informations and therefore later, access your device's local storage to retrieve/overwrite those information if you need to update/upgrade or get new result based on your inquiry.
  3. We WILL NOT share and/or save any of your personal information for example: Email address, mobile number or facebook account, detailed result of Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations with any third party.

Access and updating your information:

  1. Accessing your information: As we do not store your information in our databases, all your informations are stored in your devices. We will access your information if and only if you need to upgrade your information.
  2. Updating your information: We will save only a limited number of information in your devices and therefore for further use, we may need to overwrite the oldest information of your Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) examinations. In our beta version, we are unable to allow you to delete your account or update your password. However from our beta version, we will remove accounts which are six months old in order to control traffic in our server.
